Train for Server+.
Walk in exam-ready.

A comprehensive, self-paced, open-source training ground for the CompTIA Server+ SK0-005 certification — the vendor-neutral credential for installing, configuring, securing, and troubleshooting servers. In-depth coverage of every exam domain, Rapid Recall flashcards on a spaced-repetition scheduler, a large situational question bank, performance-based simulators plus a drag-and-drop Technical Taxonomy Mapping engine, hands-on sandbox labs, a full-length timed mock exam, and an Industry Certification Explorer.

Authored & designed by Professor Rizwan Virani

How to use this platform

  1. Read the Exam Mechanics & Career Guidance cards first. Understand the high-stakes landscape: scoring, timing, question styles, and where Server+ sits for a server/infrastructure professional.
  2. Deep-dive the four Domain Study cards. Each opens a rigorous, scenario-driven reading interface that mirrors a full prep manual, mapped to the official objectives.
  3. Lock in the terms with Rapid Recall Flashcards. 100 cards per domain plus a Master Acronym Drill, powered by spaced repetition — grade each card honestly and the scheduler resurfaces what you're forgetting and retires what you know.
  4. Validate your knowledge with the Domain Quizzes. Ten focused questions per domain, drawn at random and graded with full rationale.
  5. Train practical skills in the PBQ Simulators. 30 interactive performance-based questions across RAID/storage, OS & network provisioning, high availability & virtualization, hardening & IAM, and troubleshooting/disaster recovery, plus a drag-and-drop technical taxonomy mapping engine that has you sort real-world scenarios into the correct categories.
  6. Benchmark readiness with the full-length, timed Mock Exam. 90 questions weighted exactly to the official domain percentages, with a countdown timer and a scoring dashboard.
  7. Get hands-on in the Labs. Twenty interactive sandbox labs spanning every exam domain — flip each card to review its objectives, then launch a full-screen console with live terminal commands, configuration panels, and a SYS_ADMIN log.
  8. Plan your path in the Supplemental Learning Hub. Open the Industry Certification Explorer to compare 39 industry certifications across 12 vendors, with costs, exam blueprints, DoD 8140 mapping, and salary and role insights.

Notice: This platform is optimized for desktop or laptop computers running the Google Chrome browser. Interactive elements, including the hands-on labs, terminal simulations, and drag-and-drop activities, require a mouse and keyboard to function correctly. These features may not display or operate properly on mobile devices.
